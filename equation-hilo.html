How to Play

Goal: Build an equation using 3–5 of your cards targeting 1 (Lo) or 20 (Hi).

Build: Click a card, then an operator, then another card, etc. Use ( ) for grouping.

Bet: Check (free), Bet/Raise (add chips), or Fold (give up).

Draw: After betting, discard up to 2 cards for new ones.

Win: Pot splits — best Hi wins half, best Lo wins half. Ties go to more cards used, then highest card.

Scoop: Win both Hi and Lo with different card subsets to take the whole pot!
